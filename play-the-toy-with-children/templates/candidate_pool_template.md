## Promotion Rules

- Promote to confirmed only after source and relevance evidence pass the
  required gates for the current intent mode.
- Keep important but inaccessible papers as unconfirmed, with next action and
  omission risk.
- Move out-of-scope or duplicate papers to excluded with a reason.
